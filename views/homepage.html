No Sleep


No sleep is a windows application designed to easily prevent your computer from going to sleep or turning off the display. This was modeled after the mac application caffeine.

Puppy Cam


Puppycam.xyz is a simple web application designed for realtime monitoring of our puppy Ollie while we are away at work. Video feed is from a raspberry pi connected to the home network, livestreamed into a html website with bootstrap ui.
